Cat

Cats spend an average of 13 to 14 hours a day sleeping.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 64 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 15.1 years
  • Habitat: No specific habitat.

Cats rub up against your leg to make you smell like a cat. The more you smell like them the more they like being around you. Also, they are letting other cats know that you are their own special friend.
